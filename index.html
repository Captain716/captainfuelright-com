Who We Are

The Captain's charge: deliver ultra-clean fuel, at the tank and beyond.

More than two decades ago, the mystery behind unwanted sludge and microbial contamination in diesel fuel was finally cracked by materials science. That research became Fuel Right — a family of additive technologies that dissolve sludge, drive water out, and keep fuel systems running at peak performance.

Captain Fuel Right carries that proven science into the field. We are the ambassador-level delivery partner for fleets, generators, and marine operators who cannot afford contamination, cannot afford downtime, and cannot afford to guess.

Fuel Right technology extends benefits far beyond the tank — through filters, injectors, pumps, and combustion itself. Today there are representatives worldwide delivering ultra-clean fuel technology to the industry.

Our mission: develop and deliver innovative fuel-additive technologies that raise fuel quality and cleanliness to a standard exceeding the Worldwide Fuel Charter ISO 4406 18/16/13.

The Captain is USCG retired, Battelle-trained, and trusted by federal, state, and commercial operators who know that clean combustion is a mission requirement, not a marketing line.

The Science

Measurable. Auditable. Repeatable.

ISO 4406 18/16/13

8× Cleaner

Standard allows up to 2,500 particles/mL and 200 ppm water. Captain Fuel Right programs have delivered fuel 8× cleaner than the ISO 4406 standard in the field — reducing particulate counts from 2,500 to as low as 320 particles/mL and water content as low as 95 ppm. Some programs have achieved better than ISO 4406 13/10/8.

ASTM D2274 Sludge Formation

0.5 mg / 100 mL

FR 30K third-party ASTM D2274 accelerated-aging result: 0.5 mg/100 mL — roughly three times better than the industry threshold. Independent evidence that the treatment does what the bottle says.

Mechanism of Action

Dissolves suspended sludge and drives water to the bottom of the tank for removal. Scrubs away degraded-fuel deposits and sludge adhered to tank walls. Deposits a microscopic corrosion coating that protects the entire fuel system. Results in clean injectors, improved lubricity, enhanced atomization, better fuel economy, and reduced emissions.

Fuel Compatibility

Effective across ULSD, unleaded gasoline, MGO, IFO, VLSFO, heavy fuel oil, and bio-fuel blends. Efficacy actually improves up the fuel ladder — from residual to distillate to unleaded — so nothing in your fleet is outside the scope of the program.
