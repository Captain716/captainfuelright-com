Who We Are

The Captain's charge: deliver ultra-clean fuel, at the tank and beyond.

More than two decades ago, the mystery behind unwanted sludge and microbial contamination in diesel fuel was finally cracked by materials science. That research became Fuel Right -- a family of additive technologies that dissolve sludge, drive water out, and keep fuel systems running at peak performance.

Captain Fuel Right carries that proven science into the field. We are the ambassador-level delivery partner for fleets, generators, and marine operators who cannot afford contamination, cannot afford downtime, and cannot afford to guess.

Fuel Right technology extends benefits far beyond the tank -- through filters, injectors, pumps, and combustion itself. Today there are representatives worldwide delivering ultra-clean fuel technology to the industry.

Our mission: develop and deliver innovative fuel-additive technologies that raise fuel quality and cleanliness to a standard exceeding the Worldwide Fuel Charter ISO 4406 18/16/13.

The Captain is USCG retired, Battelle-trained, and trusted by federal, state, and commercial operators who know that clean combustion is a mission requirement, not a marketing line.

Across the Fuel Ladder

One chemistry. Every fraction of the barrel.

Crude oil refines into a ladder of fuels -- from heavy residual at the bottom to unleaded gasoline near the top. Fuel Right is effective across every combustion fuel on the ladder, and efficacy improves as you move up: residual → distillate → unleaded. Nothing in a modern fleet, vessel, or generator plant falls outside the program.

The Science

Measurable. Auditable. Repeatable.

ISO 4406 18/16/13

8× Cleaner

Standard allows up to 2,500 particles/mL and 200 ppm water. Captain Fuel Right programs have delivered fuel 8× cleaner than the ISO 4406 standard in the field -- reducing particulate counts from 2,500 to as low as 320 particles/mL and water content as low as 95 ppm. Some programs have achieved better than ISO 4406 13/10/8.

ASTM D2274 Sludge Formation

0.5 mg / 100 mL

FR 30K third-party ASTM D2274 accelerated-aging result: 0.5 mg/100 mL -- roughly three times better than the industry threshold. Independent evidence that the treatment does what the bottle says.

Mechanism of Action

Filming Amine Technology. Dissolves suspended sludge and drives water to the bottom of the tank for removal. Scrubs away degraded-fuel deposits and sludge adhered to tank walls. Deposits a microscopic corrosion-inhibiting coating that protects the entire fuel system. Breaks down suspended particles so they burn more completely -- operators see a noticeable reduction in particulate at the exhaust. Acts as an excellent surfactant and enhances the functionality of lubricity additives.

Fuel Compatibility

Effective across ULSD, unleaded gasoline, MGO, IFO, VLSFO, heavy fuel oil, and bio-fuel blends. Efficacy actually improves up the fuel ladder -- from residual to distillate to unleaded -- so nothing in your fleet is outside the scope of the program.

Frequently Asked

Questions from fleets, generators, and marine operators.

What fuels does Fuel Right work with?

Fuel Right is effective across ULSD, unleaded gasoline, MGO, IFO, VLSFO, heavy fuel oil, and bio-fuel blends. Efficacy improves as you move up the fuel ladder -- residual to distillate to unleaded -- so nothing in a typical fleet falls outside the program's scope.

What are the treatment ratios?

FR 30K treats at 1:30,000. FR 20K at 1:20,000. FR 15K at 1:15,000. ProActive at 1:20,000 (continuous dose). Winter series ratios vary by formulation and temperature target; contact the Captain for program-specific dosing.

Does Fuel Right work across all fuel grades?

Yes. Crude oil refines into a ladder of fuels -- from heavy residual at the bottom to unleaded gasoline near the top. Fuel Right is effective across every combustion fuel on the ladder (ULSD, MGO, IFO, VLSFO, HFO, unleaded, bio-fuel blends) and efficacy improves as you move up the ladder. See the Fuel Ladder section for the visual.

Is Fuel Right third-party tested?

Yes. FR 30K has a third-party ASTM D2274 accelerated-aging result of 0.5 mg / 100 mL -- roughly three times better than the industry threshold. Programs have delivered fuel 8× cleaner than the ISO 4406 18/16/13 standard in the field.

Does it work in cold weather?

Yes. The Cold Flow & Winter Series (Winter 2K / 2.8K / 4K / Cold Flow / Thaw / Winter MAX) is built for low-temperature fuel integrity in cold-storage operations, on the water, and in backup power systems where freezing failure is not an option.

Is it safe for modern diesel emissions systems?

Fuel Right's mechanism is tank-first -- dissolve sludge, remove water, protect the fuel system. The program is complementary to aftertreatment; it does not replace SCR, DPF, or DEF systems. For equipment with specific aftertreatment certifications, contact the Captain for engine-family-specific guidance.

Who else uses Fuel Right?

Approved by and used on VDOT (Virginia DOT) and MDE (Maryland Department of the Environment) fleet programs, plus commercial marine fleets, stand-by power contractors, data-center generator programs, and premium C-store fuel operators. Additional references available on request under appropriate agreements.

What compliance standards does Fuel Right meet?

Fuel Right is REACH compliant (EU chemical regulation). Programs target and exceed the Worldwide Fuel Charter ISO 4406 18/16/13 specification. Third-party ASTM D2274 testing validates sludge-formation performance. Safety Data Sheets are available on request.
